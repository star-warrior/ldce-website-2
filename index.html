About Us
About Us

L. D. College of Engineering (LDCE), is a premier government engineering institute in Gujarat set with the objectives of imparting higher education, research and training in various fields of engineering & technology.

  • Mission
  • Vision
Mission

To impart affordable and quality education in order to meet the needs of industries and achieve excellence in teaching-learning process.To create a conducive research ambience that drives innovation and nurtures research-oriented scholars and outstanding professionals.To collaborate with other academic & research institutes as well as industries in order to strengthen education and multidisciplinary research.

Read More
Vision

To contribute for sustainable development of nation through achieving excellence in technical education and research while facilitating transformation of students into responsible citizens and competent professionals.To promote equitable and harmonious growth of students, academicians, staff, society and industries, thereby becoming a center of excellence in technical education.
